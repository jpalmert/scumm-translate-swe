## DETAILED FINDINGS BY CATEGORY

### PIRATE/NAUTICAL TERMS

#### pirate → pirat
**Status:** ✓ CORRECT, but consider alternative  
**Research:** Both "pirat" and "sjörövare" are valid Swedish terms.
- **pirat** - International loanword (from Latin *pirata*, used in Swedish since 1644)
- **sjörövare** - Native Swedish compound "sea robber" (sjö + rövare)

**Recommendation:** Consider using **"sjörövare"** for more colorful, authentic Swedish flavor. Pirates of the Caribbean Swedish Wikipedia uses both terms. "Sjörövare" sounds more traditional and evocative.

**Additional Swedish pirate terms found:**
- **buckanjär** - buccaneer (specific to Caribbean pirates from Hispaniola)
- **flibustiär** - freebooter (alternate buccaneer term)
- **kapare** - privateer/corsair (licensed raider with government authorization)
- **fribytare** - freebooter (general term)
- **korsar** - corsair

#### ship → skepp
**Status:** ✓ EXCELLENT  
**Research:** "Skepp" is the standard Swedish term for a large vessel capable of extended voyages. Historically refers to three-masted sailing ships.

**Synonyms found:** fartyg (vessel - more formal), båt (boat - too small), skuta (schooner - specific type), farkost (craft - generic)

**Recommendation:** Keep "skepp" - perfect for pirate ships.

**Issue:** Modern Swedish speakers might think "celebrity" first, not "statue."

**Recommendation:** Consider **"Avgud"** or **"Staty"** to be clearer:
- **"Den Månghändiga Avguden"** (The Many-Handed False God)

#### curse → förbannelse
**Status:** ✗ **INCORRECT** - MAJOR ISSUE  
**Research:** "Förbannelse" primarily means verbal cursing/condemnation (yttrande, ed där man förbannar någon = utterance or oath where one curses someone). It's about the ACT of cursing (swearing/damning), not a magical affliction.

**Synonyms listed:** fördömelse (condemnation), bannlysning (excommunication), svordom (profanity), svärord (curse words)

**Better alternatives for MAGICAL CURSE:**
- **"förtrollning"** - bewitchment/enchantment
- **"trolldom"** - witchcraft/sorcery
- **"hex"** - hex (borrowed from English)
- **"ond trolldom"** - evil sorcery

**Recommendation:** **CHANGE** from "förbannelse" to **"förtrollning"** or **"ond trolldom"** for magical curses. "Förbannelse" is only correct if someone is literally shouting curses (swearing), not for a magical hex.
